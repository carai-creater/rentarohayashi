Profile

From past steps to what’s next

Born in 1999. I played soccer from elementary school and went through Sagan Tosu’s youth system up to U18. After that, I shifted my focus to study, starting almost from zero; in 2021 I entered the Saga University School of Medicine. Along the way I launched local events, led Idai Juku as its representative, and served as university festival committee chair. In 2023 I started working with AI and RAG and founded CRAai Inc., to explore new ways of building business and infrastructure.

Strong fields: English, Chemistry · What I enjoy: Soccer, running
